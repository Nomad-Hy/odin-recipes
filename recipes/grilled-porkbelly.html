Grilled-porkbelly

Description

Samgyeopsal is a popular Korean dish made from the pork belly, which is the abdominal part of the pig. It features layers of fat and meat, offering a juicy and tender texture. Typically seasoned with just salt and pepper, it is grilled over charcoal or a gas stove. It is usually enjoyed wrapped in lettuce leaves with various accompaniments like garlic, ssamjang (a savory dipping sauce), and chili peppers. While delicious on its own, these additions enhance its rich flavor even more.

Ingredient

    Pork belly:500 grams

    Salt:1 teaspoon

    Black pepper:1/2 teaspoon

    Lettuce leaves:1 head

    Garlic:4~5 cloves

    Ssamjang:3~4 tablespoons

    Chili peppers:2~3

Step

    Prepare the Ingredients:

      Slice the pork belly into bite-sized pieces.

      Wash and dry the lettuce leaves.

      slice garlic and chili peppers.

    Season the Pork Belly

      Sprinkle salt and black pepper evenly over the pork belly slices.

    Heat the Grill

      Place the pork belly slices on the grill or pan.

      Cook for 3~4 minutes on each side, or until the meat is crispy and cooked through.

    Prepare for Serving

      Arrange the grilled pork belly on a serving plate.

      Serve with lettuce leaves, sliced garlic, chili peppers, and ssamjang on the side.
