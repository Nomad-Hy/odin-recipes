Soysauce-Egg-Rice

Description

Soy sauce egg rice, or "Ganjang Gyeran Bap," is a simple and comforting Korean dish. It consists of steamed rice topped with a raw or lightly cooked egg, which is then seasoned with soy sauce and often a bit of sesame oil.

The egg is mixed into the hot rice, creating a savory and rich flavor. It's typically enjoyed as a quick meal or snack, valued for its ease of preparation and satisfying taste.

Ingredient

    steamed rice: 1 cup

    Egg: 1

    Soy sauce:1~2 tablespoons

    Sesame oil:1 teaspoon

    Salt: to taste

    Black pepper: to taste

Step

    Prepare the Rice:

      Cook 1 cup of rice and keep it warm.

    Prepare the Egg:

      Crack 1 egg into a small bowl. You can use it raw or lightly cook it in a pan.

    Season the Rice:

      Place the warm rice in a bowl.

    Add the Egg:

      Pour the egg over the rice.

    Add Soy Sauce:

      Drizzle 1~2 tablespoons of soy sauce over the egg and rice.

    Optional Additions:

      Add 1 teaspoon of sesame oil and season with salt and black pepper to taste if desired.
