Soysauce-Egg-Rice

Description

Soy sauce egg rice, or "Ganjang Gyeran Bap," is a simple and comforting Korean dish. It consists of steamed rice topped with a raw or lightly cooked egg, which is then seasoned with soy sauce and often a bit of sesame oil.

The egg is mixed into the hot rice, creating a savory and rich flavor. It's typically enjoyed as a quick meal or snack, valued for its ease of preparation and satisfying taste.

Ingredient

    steamed rice: 1 cup

    Egg: 1

    Soy sauce:1~2 tablespoons

    Sesame oil:1 teaspoon

    Salt: to taste

    Black pepper: to taste
